## Sending tips

**Personalise before sending**
- Name the specific thing you like about their space in line 1
- If you've actually been there, say so — it changes the tone completely
- Don't send the same text to 20 places at once; send 5, see what lands

**Channel choice**
- Instagram DM → best for galleries and aesthetic venues
- Telegram → best for expat-run spaces and community hubs
- Email → best for established bars, cultural centers, co-working spaces

**Follow-up (after 5–7 days, no reply)**
> Hi [name], just checking in — did you get a chance to see my message? Happy to answer any questions or just share the deck link if easier. — Nelli

**Objections you'll hear + short answers**
- *"We don't have enough wall space"* → "We only need 6–7 A3 sheets — can hang on a door, a pillar, anywhere that's visible. 30 cm of wall per sheet."
- *"We already do events"* → "This would be monthly at most, always on a slot that works for you. It's meant to complement what you already run, not compete."
- *"What if not enough people come?"* → "I handle all applications and only confirm the session once we have 6+ people. I won't leave you with an empty room."
- *"Is it noisy / disruptive?"* → "The format is actually quiet — people speak softly with their partner, there's no lecturing or microphone. Ambient noise level."
