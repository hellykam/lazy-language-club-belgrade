01 · what it is

Not a class — a tasting.

An evening where guests sample several languages: hear a native in the headphones, repeat, then chat with a partner — and swap. Guided by Nelli, who's run a language club for 2.5 years (hundreds of evenings).

1

Listen 🎧

Scan a QR, a native reads the line into your headphones.

2

Shadow

Repeat out loud — catch the sound, no grammar drills.

3

Speak

Run the dialogue with a partner, then swap partners.

02 · what we need from you

You host. We run it.

  • A cozy corner for up to 10 people
  • One weekend slot (Sat evening or Sun brunch)
  • A shout-out to your community (one post / story)

We bring the method, the printed sheets, the native audio, and the energy.

03 · what it brings you

A cultural event that pays its way.

  • Fills quiet hours with a recurring, on-brand event
  • Draws an engaged, paying crowd of expats + locals — drinks & food sales
  • Fresh, photogenic content for your socials
  • Positions your space as a real community hub
04 · the bigger picture

A small bridge between expats & locals.

People who live side by side in Belgrade, actually talking — in several languages, over a drink. Let's run one pilot evening together and see the room light up.
