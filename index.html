It's not a course. It's a language tasting.

You don't sign up to "finally learn Serbian in 4 months." You come to taste languages — like a wine tasting, but for your mouth and your brain. In four cozy evenings you'll speak real phrases in several languages, find out which one clicks for you, and meet people who love this stuff too. No textbooks. No grammar drills. No one puts you on the spot.

how it works

The native is in your ears. 🎧

Each language lives on its own A3 dialogue sheet, displayed on the gallery walls like artwork. Scan the QR code and a native speaker reads it straight into your headphones — so you're never guessing how it should sound.

1

Listen

Scan the QR code on the dialogue sheet — a native speaker reads it straight into your headphones.

2

Shadow

Repeat out loud, copy the melody. No grammar lectures — just the sound and the rhythm.

3

Play it live

Run the dialogue with a partner, swap roles, swap partners. Your guide keeps the room moving.

Bring your own headphones — it's the one thing we can't lend you. You'll leave with a few phrases you can actually say, and your stamped language passport.

honestly

Who it's for

This is for you if you…

  • get nervous speaking and want a soft, safe place to start
  • studied a language once and forgot most of it
  • are curious about several languages, not married to one
  • find traditional courses heavy and slow
  • just moved to Belgrade and want to dip into Serbian

This is not for you if you…

  • need intensive, exam-level study of a single language
  • want fluency by next month

If you've ever thought "wait, I could actually do this" — you're exactly who we made this for.

good questions

FAQ

I want to focus on just one language — is this for me?

You'll get more out of it picking two, but you can lean hard into one and just sample the rest. The magic is in switching.

Can I join if I'm a total beginner in everything?

Yes. Zero level is the default setting here.

You're not a native speaker — why should I trust you?

You're not learning pronunciation from me — the natives are in your headphones. I'm your guide: I bring the method, keep it fun, and make sure nobody freezes. My proof is my own result and two years of running this.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just headphones (any kind) and yourself.

Is there homework?

No. Never.

What if my languages don't match the group?

We balance every cohort from the applications so there are always enough partners for each language. That's why the form asks what you want to practice and what you already speak.

Where and when?

A small gallery on Balkanska 44, Belgrade. Weekend sessions — Saturday evening or Sunday brunch time (we confirm with the group).
